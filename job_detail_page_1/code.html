Senior Product Designer

India's Best - Bangalore, India (Hybrid)

Job Description

We are seeking a visionary Senior Product Designer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be instrumental in shaping the user experience of our flagship products, from conceptualization to execution. You'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to create intuitive, innovative, and beautiful interfaces that address complex user challenges. If you have a passion for design, a user-centric mindset, and a desire to make a significant impact, we want to hear from you.

Responsibilities

  • Lead design projects across the entire product lifecycle, setting the vision for the user experience.
  • Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and researchers to define and solve user problems.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups to communicate design ideas.
  • Conduct user research and usability testing to validate design decisions.
  • Mentor junior designers and contribute to our design system and processes.
  • Advocate for user-centered design principles and best practices throughout the organization.

Qualifications & Skills

  • 5+ years of experience in product design, with a strong portfolio showcasing your work.
  • Proficiency in design tools such as Figma, Sketch, and Adobe Creative Suite.
  • Experience with user research methodologies and data-driven design.
  • Excellent communication, presentation, and interpersonal skills.
  • A deep understanding of mobile-first and responsive design principles.
  • Ability to think at a high level about product strategy and vision.
