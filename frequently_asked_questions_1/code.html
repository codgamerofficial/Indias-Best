How is the college ranking calculated?

expand_more

Our rankings are calculated using a proprietary algorithm that considers multiple factors including academic reputation, faculty-student ratio, employer reputation, and research citations. We update our data annually to provide the most accurate and up-to-date information.

What is the application process for top universities?

expand_more

The application process for top universities typically involves submitting an online application, standardized test scores (like SAT or ACT), letters of recommendation, personal essays, and academic transcripts. Deadlines and specific requirements vary by institution, so it's crucial to check each university's official admissions website.

How do I reset my password?

expand_more

To reset your password, click on the "Log In" button, and then select the "Forgot Password?" link. You will be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account. A password reset link will be sent to your email with further instructions.

The website is not loading correctly, what should I do?

expand_more

If you are experiencing technical difficulties, please try clearing your browser's cache and cookies. If the problem persists, ensure your browser is up to date. For further assistance, please reach out to our technical support team via the "Contact Us" page, providing details about the issue and the browser you are using.
